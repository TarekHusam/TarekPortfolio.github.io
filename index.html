Bike Sales

In this project, I have employed a diverse dataset sourced from Google to develop a comprehensive Bike Sales Data analysis project using Excel, SQL, Tableau ,and Power BI.

The primary objective of this analysis is to provide a comprehensive overview of the sales activities within the company and gain valuable insights into the trends that occurred in sales volume from 2016 to 2018. The management seeks to obtain a detailed breakdown of revenue per region, store, product, category, and brand. To achieve this, a combination of SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau will be employed, executing the following systematic steps:

Steps:

  1. Data Understanding: Begin by thoroughly understanding the requirements and expectations of the management regarding the sales analysis. Define the key metrics and performance indicators needed to assess the sales activities effectively.
  2. Data Collection: Gather and compile the relevant dataset, encompassing vital information such as sales transactions, customer demographics, bike models, pricing, and other pertinent variables.
  3. Data Preprocessing: Conduct a thorough data cleansing process to address any inconsistencies, inaccuracies, or missing values, ensuring the data is pristine and suitable for further analysis.
  4. Data Integration: Merge, join, or link datasets from various sources, if applicable, to enrich the dataset and gain comprehensive insights using SQL.
  5. Data Analysis with SQL: Utilize SQL to perform exploratory data analysis (EDA), derive key metrics, and extract valuable patterns and trends from the dataset.
  6. Data Manipulation with Excel: Employ Microsoft Excel to organize, summarize, and visualize the data through the creation of pivot tables, charts, and graphs.
  7. -Data Visualization Tableau and Excel: Leverage those tools, powerful business intelligence tools, to construct interactive and visually appealing dashboards and reports that effectively communicate the insights derived from the analysis.
    -Advanced Data Visualization with Power Bi: utilized Power Bi to further enhance the visual representation of the data, providing additional perspectives and actionable insights to stakeholders.
